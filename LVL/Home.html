USE PAST EXAMS TO STUDY

Using exams for your classes from previous years has been a key study strategy at universities for many years now, but oftentimes the only students who have access to test banks are the ones in fraternities, sororities, or other groups on campus. Even then they are often old and outdated. With this website we are trying to create equal access to up-to-date study material for all students.
